Bonaventure at its core

What’s truly important to us as an organization? Bonaventure’s core values define who we are as a company. They serve as a guiding principle in developing our culture, our brand and our business strategies. They reflect what is important to us an an organization. These are the ten core values that we live by:

  1. Choose your attitude
  2. Work hard, play hard
  3. Love each other like family, because we are
  4. Fun with a little quirkiness
  5. Make it happen
  6. Be genuine
  7. Empower and trust each other
  8. Value individuality while embracing teamwork
  9. Pride without ego
  10. Have passion
